The Jewels for the Eyes

Vibrant animals fill the tank with beautiful colors bringing your tank to life. Each creature whether fish, shrimp, or snail bring a new life to your ecosystem.

Each Jewel

    Fish.

    For every tank fish are a must. They steal the show with their bright colors and quick movements. Guppies are a great starter fish!

    Shrimp

    Little shrimp bring about new faces to a tank. They eat biofilm and leftover food. They fill your aquarium floor with color as they dart around the tank.

    Snails.

    Snails are essential for every tank. These guys will scale walls and eat like theres no tommorow.
