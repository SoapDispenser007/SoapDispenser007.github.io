The Wealth of a Tank

A small aquarium may look simple, but inside it exists an entire ecosystem. Fish swim, shrimp and snails graze, plants grow, and billions of microscopic organisms work constantly to keep the environment stable.

The Layers of Wealth

  • Visible life such as fish, shrimp, and snails.
  • Plants that produce oxygen and shelter.
  • Bacteria that maintain balance in the water.

Each layer contributes to the stability of the system. Together they form a living example of ecological abundance and wealth.
