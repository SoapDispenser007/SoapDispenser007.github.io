Beauty Beyond Sight

Plants bring a lush aspect to your tank and bring about new bacterias that sustain your tank even if you are unable to see them.

Bacterias

While bacteria in your tank may sound bad it is actually very beneficial to your tank ecosystem. They help grow algae and biofilm that snails and shrimp love snacking on as well as keep your tank healthy.

Beneficial Bacteria

Bacteria in your tank helps sustain a nitrogen cycle which helps convert toxic ammonia into nitrate. With too much ammonia present in your tank your water quality will fail and your animals will die.

Plants

Live plants work better than any oxygenator or air stone ever will, they naturally create bacteria and oxygen while giving a natural look to your fish tank. Fake plants are often painted and rough in a way that can harm your plants so live plants will always be better.
